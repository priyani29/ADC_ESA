PHOTOGRAPHY
Photography has been my hobby, an escape mechanism from reality for the longest time. Once I have my phone I click away at everything I see and some of the things I've pictured commonly are Clouds or the Sky, Architecture, Flowers or nature and Scenery. Every picture I click is a memory of my emotions attached to that place at that moment.
Sunsets, one of my all time favourites to picture. Sometimes, a sunset is all I need to calm down.
Architecture is the closest we will get to see history. I got capture few of them and this is one such picture.
Scenery puts into perspective who and what you are at that moment, and the emotional connection to it.
Flowers are synonymous to the variety of people we see around each us everyday. This is one such flower I've always found pretty.
